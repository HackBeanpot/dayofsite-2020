FAQs

How do I submit a project?

Please submit your project on Devpost.

Where I can I find previous projects for inspiration?

See past projects here!

Where can I find outlets, HDMIs, paper, etc?

Ask an organizer. You can reach us on Slack.

How can I find and join a team?

We’ll be holding a team formation activity at the beginning of the hackathon where you can meet potential teammates. After that, you are still welcome to join or leave a team at any time.

I have a food allergy. What should I do?

If you made note of your dietary restrictions on the confirmation page of the application portal, we’ll be sure to accommodate your needs! All food served will be labeled with allergen information, but if you have any questions, feel free to reach out to an organizer.

How do I contact a HackBeanpot organizer?

Catch one of us walking around - we’re wearing x colored shirts! Be sure you’ve joined our Slack workspace. We’ll be using it to make important announcements throughout the event.

Got any tips for presenting and talking to judges?

HackBeanpot is all about learning! We want to hear about your process - the successes as well as the challenges you’ve faced along the way. What features are you most proud of? What updates or additions would you make in the future? Did you have to step out of your comfort zone or try something new?

My project isn’t done - what do I do?

It’s totally okay if your project isn’t complete! After all, you only had a day and a half. You should still submit your project -- judges will take into account your process. You can demo as much of it as you have and talk about what you would add in the future too.

Where can I sleep?

You’ll be able to sleep in the x area labeled on the map. Remember to bring your own pillow and blanket.

Which parts of Catalant can we use?

Any areas marked in red on the map should NOT be used. Any other areas are open! There are also signs throughout the space indicated which desks are not free to use.

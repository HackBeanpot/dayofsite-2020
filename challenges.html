Kyruus Healthcare Tech Challenge

Sponsored by Kyruus

At Kyruus we work to ensure that patients can find the right doctor, for their specific need, quickly. In doing so we help them set down the path to get the best recovery/treatment available. Using publicly available datasets (such as those found on https://healthdata.gov/) what solutions can you brainstorm/hack that might alleviate an issue that is affecting the US healthcare industry today? Participate for a chance to win Kyruss SWAG and 5 brand new Hydroflasks!

Making life easier with Robotic Process Automation! Learn how to use RPA to automate tasks that would normally require tedious and repetitive manual work.
